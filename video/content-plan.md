# Video Content Plan

## 1. Main Content Pillars

### 1) Finance Reality
- Topic: salary, expenses, bank balance, money pressure
- Style: relatable voiceover + everyday visuals
- Hook examples:
  - "Salary aate hi aisa lagta hai jaise main Ambani hoon"
  - "Google Pay ka 'Payment Successful' sound ab direct dil pe chot karta hai"
- CTA: "Comment if this feels like your life"

### 2) Productivity & Laziness
- Topic: motivation, procrastination, to-do list, discipline
- Style: fast cuts, text-on-screen, POV shots
- Hook examples:
  - "Mera dimaag raat ko 2 baje duniya badal raha hota hai"
  - "Subah 8 baje wahi dimaag kehta hai, bhad me gayi duniya"
- CTA: "Save this for your lazy Sunday"

### 3) Overthinking & Brain Logic
- Topic: overthinking, self-doubt, replaying conversations, random thoughts
- Style: dramatic reaction clips, close-up face shots, voiceover
- Hook examples:
  - "Agar kisi ne 'ek baat batana' kaha toh meri heart rate fast and furious ho jaati hai"
  - "Raat ko sochte sochte full suspense thriller lagne lagta hai"
- CTA: "Tag your overthinking friend"

### 4) Social Battery & Phone Addiction
- Topic: phone usage, social media, people not replying, mental exhaustion
- Style: sitting still, staring at phone, minimal edits
- Hook examples:
  - "Mera social battery 10 minute baat karne ke baad khatam ho jaata hai"
  - "Active 1m ago dekh ke dil ko thoda sa trauma milta hai"
- CTA: "Comment 'battery low' if you relate"

### 5) Everyday College/Office Chaos
- Topic: study stress, office routine, deadlines, random life struggles
- Style: relatable daily life clips and text overlays
- Hook examples:
  - "College me sab kuchh perfect lagta hai, par andar se sab kharaab hota hai"
  - "Office ka Monday aur mera mood dono ek hi time pe crash ho jaate hain"
- CTA: "Share this with your group"

---

## 2. Weekly Posting Plan
- Monday: Finance Reality
- Wednesday: Productivity & Laziness
- Friday: Overthinking & Brain Logic
- Sunday: Social Battery or Everyday Chaos

---

## 3. Video Template Structure
Each video should follow this simple format:
1. Hook (0-3 seconds)
2. Relatable situation (3-10 seconds)
3. Punchline or twist (10-15 seconds)
4. CTA (last 2 seconds)

Example:
- Hook: "Salary aate hi aisa lagta hai..."
- Situation: showing money in hand, then expenses
- Punchline: "Phir 3 din baad sirf 10 rupee ka golgappa hi khila de"
- CTA: "Save this if you feel the same"

---

## 4. Format Ideas
- POV style
- Voiceover with text captions
- Reaction video
- Split-screen comparison
- Slow zoom + dramatic background music

---

## 5. Style Guidelines
- Length: 15-20 seconds
- Format: vertical 9:16
- Language: Hinglish
- Captions: bold and easy to read
- Music: light relatable background audio
- Editing: quick cuts, strong punchline at the end

- one strong hook
- one relatable line
- one clear CTA
