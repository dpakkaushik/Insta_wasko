# Viral-Style Video Ideas

## 1. Salary Reality
- Hook: "Salary aate hi lagta hai main future ka CEO hoon"

- Punchline: "Phir 3 din baad sirf chai aur samosa ka budget bacha rehta hai"
- CTA: "Comment 'budget broke' if you relate"

- Punchline: "Subah 8 baje wahi dimaag kehta hai, 'abhi 5 minute aur so leta hoon'"
